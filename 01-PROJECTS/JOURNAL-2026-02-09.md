**ATR×Leverage Formula**:
```python
# 14-period ATR
window = 14
data['atr'] = np.maximum(
    data['max'] - data['min'],
    abs(data['max'] - data[symbol].shift(1)),
    abs(data['min'] - data[symbol].shift(1))
)

# CRITICAL: Multiply ATR by leverage
leverage = 20  # WEEX/Bybit
atr_leverage = data['atr'] * leverage
# Result: 2% ATR becomes 40% with 20x leverage
```

2. **Van Tharp**: Expectunity = expectancy × opportunity. For Vince, opportunity (3000 trades) is key, expectancy can be near-zero because rebates add positive edge.
3. **Hilpisch Ch 11**: ATR×leverage for SL/TP positioning. 2% ATR becomes 40% with 20x leverage.
4. **Jansen**: SHAP values for per-trade failure explanation. Helps identify which signals to skip.

### For Vicky (ML - Rebates)
1. **De Prado Meta-Labeling**: XGBoost decides skip/act on Four Pillars signals. 55% skip accuracy on bad trades = survival without rebates.
2. **De Prado Bet Sizing**: Map XGBoost confidence → position size (0.5% to 2% risk). High confidence = full size, marginal = half size.

3. **Sweeney MFE/MAE**: Classify losers (A/B/C/D) to optimize BE raise. Even MORE critical with FTMO 10% drawdown limit.

## Position Sizing Formula (All Three Systems)
- **Risk per trade**: 1-2% of account
- **Stop loss = R**: Can be larger than 1-2% (e.g., 5 ATR = 10% stop)
- **Formula**: `position_size = (account × risk%) / (stop_loss_distance × price)`
- **Critical metric**: Consecutive downside maximum

**Examples**:
- **Andy (FTMO)**: $200K × 1% = $2,000 risk/trade. Max 3 losses/day (3% daily), max 10 losses total (10% total).
- **Vince (Crypto)**: $500 margin × 1% = $5 risk/trade (but $8 commission = 160% of risk). Only viable with rebates.
- **Vicky (Copy)**: Must have higher account or higher risk% to justify $8 commission without rebates. Need 55%+ win rate.
