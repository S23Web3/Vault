## Anchored VWAP - The Key Addition

**What I Was Missing:** The role of Anchored VWAP in validating trend control.

**How It Works:**
- Anchor from significant LOW for LONG setups
- Anchor from significant HIGH for SHORT setups
- Price ABOVE VWAP = Buyers in control (volume confirms buying)
- Price BELOW VWAP = Sellers in control (volume confirms selling)

**Why It Matters:**
- Combines VOLUME + PRICE into one validation tool
- Shows WHERE the actual buying/selling happened
- Not just "price going up" but "price going up WITH volume confirmation"
- Validates that one side (buyers/sellers) has control

**When Price Breaks VWAP:**
- Control is lost
- Consider exit or tighten stops
- The side that was in control no longer is
