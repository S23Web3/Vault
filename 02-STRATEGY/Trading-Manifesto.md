## 🎯 ENTRY CRITERIA

### Bull Flag (LONG)
1. Slow stoch (60-10) holding ABOVE 80
2. Medium stoch (40-4) holding ABOVE 80 (stronger) or near 80
3. Fast stoch (9-3) rotates quickly down to 20
4. Price holding ON or ABOVE 55 EMA
5. Enter as fast stoch touches/approaches 20
6. Partial profits when fast stoch crosses back above 80

### Bear Flag (SHORT)
1. Slow stoch (60-10) holding BELOW 20
2. Medium stoch (40-4) holding BELOW 20 (stronger) or near 20
3. Fast stoch (9-3) rotates quickly up to 80
4. Price holding ON or BELOW 55 EMA
5. Enter as fast stoch touches/approaches 80
6. Partial profits when fast stoch crosses back below 20

## 📉 DIVERGENCE RULES

### Bullish Divergence (LONG)
**Stage 1:**
- Stochastics UNDER 20 line
- Price makes a low, bounces, stochastics come back above 20

**Stage 2:**
- Price makes LOWER LOW (or double bottom)
- Stochastics make HIGHER LOW (staying above 20)
- Stochastics turn up

### Bearish Divergence (SHORT)
**Stage 1:**
- Stochastics OVER 80 line
- Price makes a high, pulls back, stochastics come back under 80

**Stage 2:**
- Price makes HIGHER HIGH (or double top)
- Stochastics make LOWER HIGH (staying under 80)
- Stochastics turn down
