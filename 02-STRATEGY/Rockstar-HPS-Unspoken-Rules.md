# Rockstar HPS Unspoken Rules
**Source:** John Kurisko (DayTraderRockstar) - HPS Methodology
**Added:** 2026-02-03

---

## Stochastic Bands Reference

| Stochastic | Settings | Role |
|------------|----------|------|
| Fast Stoch | 9, 3 | Small moves inside channel |
| Fast Stoch | 14, 3 | Medium confirmation |
| Fast Stoch | 40, 4 | Higher timeframe divergence |
| Full Stoch | 60, 10 | Larger channel representation |

---

## Rule 1: Entry Under Key Averages
When price is underneath the 200-period MA or VWAP and you have a buy signal, take the trade off on the first rotation above the 80-period Bollinger Band. No exceptions.

---

## Rule 2: Channel Dynamics
- Larger channel = 60-minute chart (represented by 60-10 stochastic)
- Small moves inside larger channel = 9-3 Stochastics
- In a downtrending market, always sell profits quicker

---

## Rule 3: Handling Blowthroughs
Market blows past S/R areas during news — these don't give true levels.

**Process:**
1. Wait for bounce
2. Wait for retest
3. Place trendline at the retest bounce

---

## Rule 4: Cancelling Candles
When one big down candle is immediately reversed by a big up candle (within 1-2 candles), that area gets cancelled out.

**Action:** Draw your trendline as if those candles didn't exist.

---

## Rule 5: Divergence Priority
Always take:
- **40-4 divergence** (40-period stochastic, 4 smoothing)
- Any multiple-band divergences (2+ stochastics showing divergence simultaneously)

---

## Rule 6: Divergence Channel Width Technique
1. Measure distance from Stage 1 pullback low → Stage 2 high
2. That distance = width of developing channel
3. Projects next touch point for entry/exit

---

## Notes
- These rules supplement the core HPS methodology
- 40-4 divergence carries higher conviction than 9-3 divergence
- Multiple-band divergence = strongest signal
