## 🐅 TIGER WOODS RESET PROTOCOL

> *Between each trade, you are a new person. The last trade does not exist.*

### After EVERY Trade (Win or Lose):
1. **STOP** - Step back from screens (30 seconds)
2. **BREATHE** - 4 counts in, 4 counts hold, 4 counts out (3x)
3. **RELEASE** - Say out loud: "That trade is complete"
4. **RESET** - Review: Am I still at readiness level?
5. **RETURN** - Fresh eyes, no bias from last trade

### After a LOSS:
- [ ] Logged the trade with notes
- [ ] Identified: Was it a SYSTEM loss or DISCIPLINE loss?
- [ ] If discipline loss: What triggered it?
- [ ] 5-minute walk before next trade
- [ ] Readiness re-check (still above 30?)

### After a WIN:
- [ ] Logged the trade
- [ ] No celebration until end of day
- [ ] Check: Am I getting overconfident?
- [ ] Reset to neutral
- [ ] Next trade is independent
