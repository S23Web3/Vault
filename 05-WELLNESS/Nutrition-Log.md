# 🍎 NUTRITION LOG

## Trading Nutrition Principles

Your brain is your trading tool. Feed it properly.

---

## Pre-Trading Meal (2 hours before)

**Goal:** Stable energy, no crash

### Good Options:
- Eggs + whole grain toast + avocado
- Oatmeal + nuts + berries
- Greek yogurt + granola + banana

### Avoid:
- High sugar (crash in 2 hours)
- Heavy/greasy food (brain fog)
- Empty stomach (irritability)

---

## During Trading Sessions

**Goal:** Maintain focus, avoid energy dips

### Snack Options (every 2-3 hours):
- Handful of nuts (almonds, walnuts)
- Apple + peanut butter
- Dark chocolate (small piece)
- Protein bar (low sugar)
- Cheese + whole grain crackers

### Avoid:
- Chips, candy, sweets
- Energy drinks (crash later)
- Large meals (blood diverts to digestion)

---

## Caffeine Rules

- Maximum 2-3 cups coffee per day
- Last caffeine by 14:00 (protect sleep)
- Always pair with water
- Never trade jittery
