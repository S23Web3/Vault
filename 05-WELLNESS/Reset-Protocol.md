# 🧘 TIGER WOODS RESET PROTOCOL

> "Between each trade, you are a new person. The last trade does not exist."

---

## The Philosophy

Tiger Woods famously resets completely between each hole. The previous shot - good or bad - is gone. This is how you become a maestro.

**Your trades are independent events.** A loss doesn't make the next trade more likely to win. A win doesn't mean you're "hot." Each setup is evaluated fresh.

---

## THE RESET SEQUENCE (30-60 seconds)

### 1. STOP
- Step back from screens physically
- Break eye contact with charts
- Duration: 10 seconds minimum

### 2. BREATHE
- Inhale: 4 counts
- Hold: 4 counts  
- Exhale: 4 counts
- Repeat: 3 times

### 3. RELEASE
Say out loud: **"That trade is complete."**
- If win: "Good execution. Reset."
- If loss: "Part of the game. Reset."

### 4. ASSESS
Quick self-check:
- Am I still above 30/50 readiness?
- Am I feeling revenge/FOMO?
- Is my body tense? Relax shoulders.

### 5. RETURN
- Fresh eyes on charts
- No bias from previous trade
- Wait for next valid setup

---

## AFTER A LOSS - Extended Protocol

1. Complete standard reset
2. Log the trade immediately
3. Ask: System loss or discipline loss?
4. 5-minute walk (non-negotiable)
5. Re-check readiness score
6. If below 30: Stop trading

---

## AFTER A WIN - Extended Protocol

1. Complete standard reset
2. Log the trade
3. Check: Am I overconfident now?
4. Reminder: Next trade is independent
5. No celebration until end of day

---

## Warning Signs You Need a Longer Break

- Clenched jaw or tight shoulders
- Racing thoughts
- Wanting to "make back" losses
- Feeling invincible after wins
- Checking P&L repeatedly
- Ignoring your rules "just this once"

If any of these: 15-minute break minimum.
