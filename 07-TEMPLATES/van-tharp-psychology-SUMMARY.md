## DAILY PRACTICES

1. **Morning**: Review objectives. Check if today's plan aligns with mission.
2. **Pre-trade**: Identify which biases might affect you today. Name them.
3. **During trade**: Position sizing FIRST, entry LAST. Risk % before anything.
4. **Post-trade**: Log R-multiple. Was exit discipline maintained?
5. **Weekly**: Calculate SQN. Are you improving? Review worst-case scenario.
6. **Monthly**: Belief inventory. What changed? What needs work?
